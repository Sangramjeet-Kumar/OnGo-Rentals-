Rental Policies

Driver Requirements

Renters must be at least 21 years of age with a valid driver's license held for at least 1 year. International customers must provide a valid passport and international driving permit.

Security Deposit

A security deposit of ₹1,000 - ₹10,000 (depending on vehicle type) is required at the time of pickup. The deposit will be refunded within 5-7 business days after the vehicle is returned undamaged.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 12 hours before pickup. Cancellations less than 12 hours before pickup incur a 25% charge. No-shows result in a 50% charge of the total rental amount.

Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank and should be returned with a full tank. If the vehicle is not returned with a full tank, a refueling fee will be charged.

Mileage Limits

Standard rentals include 100 kilometers per day for cars and 80 kilometers per day for bikes. Additional kilometers will be charged at ₹10-₹15 per km depending on the vehicle.
